About YuWe Fitness

I'm Yuvaraj, a fitness professional on a mission. After seven years of helping clients transform their lives, I founded YuWe Fitness. YuWe stands for 'You' and 'We,' because I believe that fitness should be for everyone, irrespective of age, gender, income, or location. At YuWe Fitness, we're committed to transforming lives through fitness. Our gym provides a welcoming and motivating environment where you can achieve your health and wellness goals.

Whate is BMI?

Body Mass Index, is a numerical measure that assesses an individual's body fat based on their weight and height. It provides a rough estimate of one's overall health and helps categorize individuals as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
